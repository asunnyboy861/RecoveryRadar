Frequently Asked Questions

How does RecoveryRadar track muscle recovery?

RecoveryRadar reads your workout data from Apple Health, identifies which muscles were trained using EMG-driven exercise mapping, and calculates recovery status using an exponential decay algorithm based on strain intensity and time elapsed.

Why is my recovery data not showing?

Make sure you've granted HealthKit access in the app's onboarding flow or in Settings. RecoveryRadar needs permission to read your workout data from Apple Health.

How do I manage my subscription?

You can manage your subscription through your Apple ID settings: Settings → Your Name → Subscriptions → RecoveryRadar. Or visit App Store Subscriptions.

Can I get a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple's report a problem page: reportaproblem.apple.com

Is my data safe?

Absolutely. All data is processed and stored locally on your device. We never transmit any data to external servers. See our Privacy Policy for details.

What's included in the free tier?

The free tier includes: front body heat map, overall recovery score, 5 major muscle group tracking, basic training suggestions, and 7-day workout history. Upgrade to Pro for all 16 muscle groups, front + back views, trends, and more.
