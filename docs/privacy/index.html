Privacy Policy

RecoveryRadar - Smart Muscle Recovery Tracker

Last updated: May 4, 2026

Your privacy is our priority. RecoveryRadar processes all data on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

Data We Access

RecoveryRadar accesses the following data through Apple HealthKit (read-only):

  • Workout data (type, duration, calories)
  • Heart rate data
  • Heart rate variability (HRV) data
  • Sleep analysis data

This data is used solely to calculate muscle recovery status and provide training suggestions. All data remains on your device.

Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is transmitted to any external server, cloud service, or third party.

No Account Required

RecoveryRadar does not require user registration, login, or account creation. We do not collect email addresses, names, or any identifying information.

No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or distribute any user data to third parties. There are no analytics services, advertising networks, or tracking tools integrated into the app.

HealthKit Data Usage

HealthKit data is accessed only with your explicit permission. You can revoke access at any time through the Settings app on your iPhone. RecoveryRadar only reads data from HealthKit; it never writes data to HealthKit.

Subscription Data

Subscription management is handled by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Subscription status is verified through Apple's StoreKit framework.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, deleting the app will permanently remove all your data. There is no server-side data to delete.

Children's Privacy

RecoveryRadar is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
